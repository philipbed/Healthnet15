About us

Healthcare, simplified.

In the modern age, dealing with your health shouldn't be complicated. At HealthNet, we empower users to connect directly with their healthcare professionals. With HealthNet, you can take your health in your own hands.

Features

Medical history

Keep track of your medical history, and share it with your doctor. Everyone's on the same page.

Appointments

Directly schedule appointments with your doctor. No phone calls, no fuss.

Prescriptions

Quickly refill prescriptions. It's as easy as 1, 2, 3.

Messaging

Directly message your doctor, without a need for phone calls or visits. Call me, never.

Your information, secured.

HealthNet is a health application that puts your security first. Doctors, nurses, and patients have a unique experience tailored to them. Your private information is only shared between you and your trusted medical professionals.
